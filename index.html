Game Description:
Chicken Invaders is a single-round spaceship shooter game where you control one spaceship and face off against an army of 20 enemy chickens piloting spaceships arranged in 4 rows of 5. Use your customized movement and firing keys to dodge enemy fire and eliminate all spaceships before time runs out. Fast reflexes and precision aim are key to victory!

Game Configuration:
Before launching the game, players can customize the following settings in the configuration screen:

  • Game Length: Choose how many minutes the game lasts.
  • Movement Keys: Assign custom keys for left, right, up, and down movement.
  • Firing Key: Select your preferred key for shooting beams.
  • Beam Color: Choose your laser beam color using the color picker.
  • Sound Effects: Toggle epic background music and sound effects on or off.
  • Spaceship Selection: Pick your spaceship design by clicking on one of the available ship images.

Goal:
Shoot down all enemy ships before the timer runs out or your lives are gone. Watch your score and aim for the top!
